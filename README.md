Instructions: 

- Run the server
- connect 2 clients
- Wait for the text to say "Ready". That means that two players have connected.
- Once the text changes to "Ready", pick rock/paper/scissors and press the corresponding button to lock in your answer.
- Wait till your opponent also locks in their answer and the result will be displayed on the screen.
- DO NOT wait for the text to change to "try again" or whatever, just lock in your answer again and wait for your opponent to do the same.
- Rinse and repeat until you want to stop playing.
